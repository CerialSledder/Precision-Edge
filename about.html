About Precision Edge

More to the point, it’s not about us.  It’s about quality.  Since the beginning in 1989, our goal has been creating and manufacturing products of the highest quality and with the sharpest cutting edges in the industry.  When surgeons are using one of our products, we want them to have full confidence in its performance.  That’s our mission.

From our first days as a bur-manufacturing company, our mantra has been quality.  This led to an expanded product line and a much larger building, which we also outgrew.  Now with two full-service facilities in Northern Michigan, we not only continue our commitment to quality, but have the capacity to do even more.

We employ talented people who design, develop and manufacture burs, blades, drills, reamers, taps, screws, rasps, instrumentation and other surgical accessories for the orthopedic, ENT, neurology, podiatry, arthroscopy and plastic surgery disciplines.  We put forth the effort in making sure our products are the best.

Mission Statement

Precision Edge Surgical Products is dedicated to design and manufacture of surgical tools and accessories that are second to none in quality while providing exceptional value for our customers.

Location

Precision Edge has two locations in beautiful Northern Michigan: Sault Ste. Marie, located in the Upper Peninsula, on the St. Mary’s River which connects Lake Superior with Lake Huron and Boyne City, located in the Northern Lower Peninsula, just two hours south of Sault Ste. Marie on the shores of Lake Charlevoix, which is connected to Lake Michigan. Both locations are year-round playgrounds for four season enthusiasts.
